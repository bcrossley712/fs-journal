## Questions

1. What is a User Experience? What should be considered when developing a User Experience?
A user experience is any interaction a user has with your side. You must consider the users feelings when building out your application. 
2. What are some benefits of taking into account the User Experience?
It can make it more pleasing for the user and they are more likely to stick around and become potential customers/clients.
3. Why is the User Experience important?
The ultimate goal of what you are building is to get someone to use it.  You can build all the mundane applications in the world but if no one wants to use them, it's your loss.
